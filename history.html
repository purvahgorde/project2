The History of Infosys

Tracing the journey of one of the world's most influential technology and consulting companies.

Key Milestones

1981 - Foundation

Infosys was founded in Pune, India, by seven engineers with a vision to leverage technology to drive business transformation.

1993 - IPO

Infosys went public, marking a significant milestone and establishing itself as a leader in technology services.

2000s - Global Expansion

Infosys expanded its global footprint, opening offices and innovation centers worldwide.

About Infosys

Infosys is a global leader in technology services and consulting, enabling enterprises in more than 50 countries to navigate their digital transformation.
